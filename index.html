VoiceBridge: Bilingual Web-Based AAC Platform for Non-Speaking Children Worldwide
TechRxiv Preprint | November 2025 | Bahrain
This research presents VoiceBridge, a culturally-aware bilingual (Arabic-English) augmentative and alternative communication platform developed to address the significant gap in accessible communication tools for non-speaking children in the world. The system utilizes modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Web Speech API) to create an intuitive interface for sentence construction through visual symbols, with text-to-speech output in both languages. The solution specifically addresses the needs of 2,200+ non-speaking individuals in Bahrain, with architecture designed for scalability across the world.
